🧘 fitness tips from our group

Small habits, big results. Here's what works for us:

  • Morning stretch: 5 minutes dynamic moves before coffee.
  • Hydrate: drink at least 2L water, especially on game days.
  • Rest is training: take one full rest day per week.
  • Buddy system: workout with a friend = more fun, more consistency.
